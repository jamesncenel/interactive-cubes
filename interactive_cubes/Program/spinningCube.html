Interactive Cubes

Author: James Lord-Ender-Laing        Date: Februrary 28th 2020
Description: This program allows a user to add rotating cubes to a canvas
Functionality:
This program allows for users to click on the canvas (indicated by the blue area) and see a cube appear on where they clicked rotating in a random direction and at a random speed. The cubes are identical in terms of proportions and colors. The 4 buttons allow the axis of rotation to change to the axis matching the button pressed.
Resources: Everything in the common folder: MV.js, initShaders.js, initShaders2.js, and webgl-utils.js.
